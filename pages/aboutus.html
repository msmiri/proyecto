About Us

Our story started with a problem (the best ideas usually do). Some ten years ago, our founder was furnishing his flat. Frustrated at the lack of well-designed, good quality and affordable sofas, he set about redefining the process. The concept was clear: collaborate with independent designers and makers to create pieces you’ll love, minus the mark-up. And so Pedraza Muebles was born. The destination for creating your dream home.
