Muebles Pedraza woods are coniferous kind domestic products. Even when seeking ways to use its gentle texture and softness, they were too brittle, making them unsuitable for furniture-making. Uniformly compressing the woods gives it a uniform strength, making it possible for the first time to use it with a straight grain.
